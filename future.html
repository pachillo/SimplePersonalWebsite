My Computing Dreams Ahead

Some thoughts

At this point in my life, the most important thing to me is to figure out what I want to do. Computer Science is a broad major, and honestly, I find almost all of them equally interesting (definitely not because of me being indecisive).


While at University

I cannot speak well about what the future holds, especially in the tech world, where everything changes rapidly. But I hope to prepare myself best to improvise and accept great opportunities when the time comes. Looking at the rest of my time in university, I am strongly interested in research, so hopefully, I can land a research internship. Moreover, another internship working on a practical project would help me a lot to see the two sides of Computer Science. For now, I have to stay locked in, focus on my studying and be the better version of myself every day.


After University

Once I graduated, I hope that I can get a stable job and working visa in Australia, that's all for now :)


My Goals while still at Uni

Ranked on a scale of 1-5, 1 is the most important and 5 is the least important.

  1. Make new friends
  2. Get an internship
  3. Achieve good grades
  4. Join exchange program to an university abroad
  5. Meet Gordon Ramsay

If things go south, I'll always have a back-up plan!
